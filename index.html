Showcase

Products
You have an app, a food, a drink, a clothing line, an accessory line, etc. that you want present to our community. Bonus points if you can offer your product for free or at a discounted rate.
Services
You do law, accounting, consulting, branding, marketing, web development, etc. and you have something of value to offer our community. Bonus points if you can offer your service for free or at a discounted rate.
Funds
You have a fund, you are part of a VC firm, you work for a lending club or a bank, etc. and want to actively invest in our community. Bonus points if you have an ability to educate our group on a specific topic relating to finance or fundraising.
We Expect
You will tell a compelling story about your business, however you will not use this stage simply as a means of promoting your business. Rather, your job is to be inspirational and informative about a major success or failure that you have encountered along your journey.
We Provide
An excited, engaged audience of your peers. We will provide all of the production needs for your talk: projector, microphone, sound system, beer.
You Get
A workshop dedicated to solving one of your most urgent business challenges. Social engagement via custom twitter and instagram hashtags. Showcase in our "Made In Brooklyn" section of this site when applicable.
